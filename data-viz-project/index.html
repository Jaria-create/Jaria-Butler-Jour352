2024 Sees Decline in DC's Violent Crime Rate: Crime Comparison

By Jaria Butler

Violent crime in the District of Columbia has seen a significant decline, reversing the upward trend observed between 2022 and 2023. From 2023 to 2024, crime rates have dropped, putting the city on track to achieve its lowest violent crime rate in nearly two decades, since the Metropolitan Police Department first began tracking these statistics.
